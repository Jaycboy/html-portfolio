The Best Movies According to Jesse

My top 3 movies of all-time

How to Train your dragon

Story of a younng boy looking to fight against the rules of a socety that did not make sense to him.

How to Train Your Dragon 2

After succesfuly changing the culture of his society, the young boy has grown into a young man who now fights to protect the peace of his society from those that look to disrupt it

How to Train Your Dragon 3

The thrilling conclusion to the trilogy where the young man has become the leader of the society and now looks to find a permanent solution to protect all that he cares about. Even if it means saying goodbye.
